I am a graduate student in Operations Research and Financial Engineering at Princeton University. I work under the supervision of Prof. Han Liu and Prof. Robert Vanderbei. Before coming to Princeton, I get my bachelor's degree from National University of Singapore under the supervision of Prof. Kim-Chuan Toh.

I am working on the problems arisen from Big Data from both statistical and computational perspectives. Currently, I am interested in high-dimensional inference and large-scale optimization problems with applications to adaptive trials and genomic data.

I will be joining the Department of Statistics and Department of Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering at Penn State University as an assistant professor in August 2016.
